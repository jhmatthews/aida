Eschaton

Posted 4 weeks ago

We’re delighted to announce that our debut album, entitled `Eschaton’, will be released on Robot Needs Home on June 2nd, 2014.

The first track can be streamed exclusively over at Musical Mathematics right now:

Go listen.

We will also be touring to coincide with the release, playing the following dates:

Friday 23rd May- Penny Theatre, Canterbury
Monday 2nd June- Avondale House, Southampton
Tuesday 3rd June- Firebug, Leicester
Wednesday 4th June - Bar 1:22, Huddersfield
Friday 6th June- Album Launch: The Facemelter, Black Heart, London
Saturday 7th June- The Ferret, Preston

The London date is our album launch in association with the magnificient Chaos Theory﻿. It’s going to be a right party.

We’re going to be playing with some great bands: Delta Sleep for Cantebury, Alpha Male Tea Party for a few dates and we’ll also be joined by our brothers Obe in London. Then we’ll be rounding off our summer by playing the mighty Arctangent. It’s going to be a right party.

Tell your friends, share, and enjoy the listen.

Aida x

Ahoy! We’re completely and totally DELIGHTED to announce that we will be playing the incredible ArcTanGent festival on 28-30 August!

This festival really is the best thing around for all your post/math/whatever-rock connoisseurs and has an awesome lineup (Russian Circles, ASIWYFA, This Will Destroy You etc.)

Get your tickets here: http://arctangent.co.uk/tickets/

http://handmadefestival.co.uk/

Onwards, comrades.
